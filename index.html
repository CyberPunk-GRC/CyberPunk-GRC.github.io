Risk Mitigation & Action Plan

Loss of company data (physical)

Plan: Secure server rooms with badge & biometric access. Action: implement within 1 month. Controls: Physical/technical.

Lost/stolen assets

Plan: Regular backups, device encryption, strong passwords, biometric options. Action: Immediate. Controls: Technical.

Production outages / DR

Plan: Multi-site redundancy across three DCs, regular DR tests. Action: within 1 week for DR prioritization. Controls: Physical/Procedural/Technical.

Internet threats / patching

Plan: Patch management, network backups, IDS/IPS, vulnerability scanning via Tenable/Nessus. Action: Immediately. Controls: Technical.

Insider threats

Plan: Least privilege, monitoring, DLP, alerts (Snort/IDS), sanctions. Action: within 1 week. Controls: Procedural & technical.

Regulatory changes

Plan: Governance committee to draft updates as needed. Action: within 6 months for policy-level changes.

Cost-Benefit (Qualitative)

Backups, biometrics, redundancy, and monitoring reduce operational risk and increase incident detection — qualitative benefits justify implementation even when a numeric ROI is not feasible.
